🍫 Statistical Study of World Cocoa Production and Price

📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive statistical analysis of world cocoa production and price trends. The study examines cocoa cultivation, production, productivity, domestic and international price behavior, export-import performance, and market instability across Kerala, India, and major cocoa-producing countries. Statistical techniques and time series analysis were applied to understand long-term trends, price volatility, and the relationship between domestic and international cocoa markets.

---

🎯 Objectives

- Study trends in cocoa area, production, and productivity.
- Analyze domestic and international cocoa price behavior.
- Measure price instability using the Cuddy–Della Valle Index (CDVI).
- Estimate Compound Annual Growth Rates (CAGR).
- Examine export and import performance of cocoa.
- Analyze the long-run relationship between domestic and international cocoa prices using cointegration analysis.
- Forecast future production and price trends using time series analysis.

🚀 Results

- Cocoa cultivation has expanded significantly in Kerala, India, and major producing countries.
- Cocoa production increased over the study period, while productivity varied across regions.
- Domestic and international cocoa prices exhibited considerable volatility.
- CAGR analysis highlighted long-term growth in area and production.
- CDVI identified instability in cocoa prices.
- ADF and cointegration analyses confirmed long-run relationships between domestic and international cocoa prices.
- Time series analysis provided insights into future cocoa production and price trends.
